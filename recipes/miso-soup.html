Miso Soup

A steaming miso soup before the main meal.

Miso soup is a traditional Japanese soup normally seen in a staple meal. It is usually made using miso paste added with dried seaweed, tofu, and green onions.

Miso paste is a fermented soybeans. Generally, it has three varieties: red, yellow, and white. The darker the paste, the more flavorful it is.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Miso Paste
  • Tofu
  • Green Onions

Steps:

  1. Bring the water to a boil.
  2. Add the tofu.
  3. Turn off the heat.
  4. Whisk in the miso paste.
  5. Add the chopped green onions.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
